FOCACCIA

By Helene Grimstad

Difficulty: Easy

#focaccia #italian #EasyRecipe

Foccacia is a relatively flat bread that we usually associate with the Italian cuisine. The delicious bread is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour 1kg
  • Yeast 50g
  • Water, lukewarm 9dl
  • Olive oil 2tbs
  • Salt 1tbs
  • Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients together to a dough, and let sit until double in size.
  2. Evenly lay the dough out on a abaking tray, and let sit for 20 minutes. Put the oven to 200'C
  3. Pour over some Extra Virgin Olive Oil, and press your fingers into the dough. Add maldon salt and finely cut rosemary.
  4. Put the baking tray in the oven for 25 minutes and enjoy with your favourite salad!
